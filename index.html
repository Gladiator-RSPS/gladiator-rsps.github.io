Welcome to Gladiator!

Gladiator is an MMORPG set in a medieval realm of adventure and conquest, where players assume the role of gladiators. By customizing their characters and engaging in epic battles, quests, and exploration of diverse areas, Gladiator offers an immersive experience that allows players to shape their destinies.

News & Updates

New minigame: Colosseum

Ajax (developer) 13 July 2023

Experience the awe-inspiring arena, also known as the Colosseum, now accessible for in-game play. Earn valuable Aureus points, which can be used to purchase arena caskets! Waste no time and uncover its wonders!

New item: Arena Caskets

Ajax (developer) 12 July 2023

A collection of arena caskets has been unveiled, spanning from wooden to legendary rarity. These caskets serve as gateways to acquiring exceptional and unparalleled items. Seize the chance to explore this new addition in the Colosseum!

New area: Penguins

Ajax (developer) 9 July 2023

Discover the enchanting penguin habitat, home to both small penguins and tank penguins. Engage with these delightful creatures and receive coveted penguin keys, which can unlock the mysterious Penguin chest.

New weapon: Scythe of Vitur

Ajax (developer) 8 July 2023

It's time to discuss the newly released weapon - the Scythe of Vitur. This formidable weapon enables players to strike creatures up to three times with a single attack.
